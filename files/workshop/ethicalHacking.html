Today, we reside in a virtual world where the internet connects us together. It has conjured the possibility of accessing information stored in others' systems. A lot of people are perplexed by the prospect of hacking and are always dubious about its legal prospects. Here is where the International Council of E-Commerce Consultants (EC-Council.) inoculates the concept of ethical hacking.

A certified ethical hacker has the imperative job to ensure security to confidential data and access information to protect the interests of his nation or organization.

This Knosys get an opportunity to share the ideas of these exemplary masterminds who work tirelessly to ensure that information remains secure and in safe hands and enjoy and frolic at the same time. This year, Knosys partners with Passion Solutions, a company that designs, develops, and deliver information security training to the government agencies, private sectors and educational institutions.
