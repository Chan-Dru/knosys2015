Call OF DUTY is a name known to any good pc gamer around the world. Anyone who has played it knows how exciting its single and multiplayer gameplays can get. At knosys we will be conducting a call of duty modern warfare 4 multiplayer competition. The game will be based on search and destroy game format and will consist of teams dueling each other to decide which team is the top gun. So pack your ammo, sharpen your knives, focus your senses and attack!!!!

Rules:

  • Each team consists of 4-5 players.
  • Teams can register themselves online or onspot 1 hour before the event starts.
  • Maximum number of teams can be registered is 8 to 12.
  • Registrations for teams will closed once the maximum number is reached.
  • Matches for the selected top 8 teams will be conducted as per the schedule given.
  • The opponent team wins the match if your team is not present 5 minutes prior to the commencement of the match.
