  • The time limit for this competition will be 45 minutes.
  • The number and nature of the problems and the evaluation criteria will be revealed along with the problem statements, at the venue.
  • Use of mobile phones or other electronic media will not be permitted
  • Any kind of unfair means will lead to disqualification
  • Participants must carry their respective college identity proof
