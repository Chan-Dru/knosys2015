    ITyuktha

    • The event will be conducted in 2 phases.

    Phase 1:

    Online Abstract Submission
    • The participants are required to submit an abstract of the paper via e-mail.
    • The abstract should be submitted as a PDF document (.pdf format) with Times New Roman font (size 12) and single spacing.
    • IEEE format should be adopted.
    • The selection in the first round will be based on the abstract submitted.
    • Results will be mailed to the team members of the selected teams.
    • The participants should send the title of the topic of presentation, participants name, college name, branch and year, phone number of participants and their email ids while mailing the abstract.

    Phase 2:

    Presentation during Knosys'15
    • The main event will be an on-stage event.
    • The teams shortlisted after phase 1 will be required to present the paper.
    • Three hard copies of the full paper are to be submitted before the presentation.
    • During the main event, the contestants will get 8-10 minutes to present their paper, at the end of which there will be a query session.
    • The decision of the judges would be final and binding.
    • The competition is open to undergraduate students of all disciplines.
    • A team can consist of up to 2 people, not necessarily from the same college.
    • Single participant teams are also welcomed.
    • No participant can be a part of more than 1 team.
    • There is no restriction on the number of teams from the same college.
    • The papers will be judged on:
      • Originality
      • Simplicity
      • Objectives
      • Methodology
      • Findings and Analysis
      • Presentation
      • Conclusions
    • Candidates should register their names in www.knosys.in before sending the abstract.

    NOTE:

    1. The judging panel reserves the rights to disqualify the participant team, if any of the rules given above are breached by any participant.
    2. It is the responsibility of the participants to keep themselves updated.
